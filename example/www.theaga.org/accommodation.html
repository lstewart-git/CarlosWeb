AGA 2024 Registration Package at Granlibakken Resort -- Registration open until Thursday 5 September

The $200 Registration fee for the meeting is included in Granlibakken's conference package.

Also included:

3 nights lodging (Sun/Mon/Tue Oct 6/7/8)
Reception and dinner Oct 6, all meals Oct 7/8, breakfast and lunch Oct 9
1 free beer, wine, or soda each evening
(additional beverages may be purchased)
High-speed resort-wide WiFi
Use of pool, hot tub, and tennis courts
Free parking

 

The Granlibakken Conference Package link is available through the Membership webpage
(after login to the Members Area, see the right hand sidebar)

You must be a member to register and submit an abstract
Cost is $20 student/trainee/retired; $45 individual

 

Costs for the entire meeting are all inclusive (no extra taxes or other fees):

Standard room (1 king or 2 queen beds, coffee maker, mini fridge)
    Single:    $977 + $200 registration fee
    Double:  $828 + $200 registration fee

Deluxe room (studio with king, queen, or 2 double beds, kitchen, sitting area, fireplace, balcony)
    Single:  $1,077 + $200 registration fee
    Double:  $895 + $200 registration fee

Guest staying double occupancy with registrant (includes food and beverage)
    $496

Meeting only (no lodging, everything else included)
    $702 + $200 registration fee

 

Note:  1 and 2 bedroom apartments are also available on the property.  Each registrant can book a room at the double (if sharing a bedroom) or single room rate, and note in the text box the type of apartment you want and who you plan to share it with.

Apartments have living areas and full kitchens, but note that the conference package includes all meals.
